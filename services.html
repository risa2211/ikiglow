Welcome to IkiGlow

"Ikiglow" is a portmanteau of two words: "Ikigai" and "Glow."

Ikigai: A Japanese term that translates to "a reason for being" or "a reason to wake up in the morning." It represents the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, what the world needs, and what you can be paid for.

Glow: Implies radiance, warmth, and a positive, shining quality.

So, "Ikiglow" combines these concepts to convey a sense of finding purpose, joy, and radiance in life. It suggests a state of well-being and fulfillment, which aligns well with the themes of mental health and positivity.
